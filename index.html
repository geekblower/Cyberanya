F.A.Q

One Stop Solution For All Your Queries

  • How can I participate in Cybernaya 2023?

    To participate, visit our registration page on the official Cybernaya website. Follow the registration instructions and ensure you meet the eligibility criteria.

  • Is Cybernaya open to students from all colleges?

    Yes, Cybernaya is open to students from any college or university. As long as you are from computer background and have a passion for computer science, you are welcome to join us!

  • What are the benefits of attending Cybernaya?

    Attending Cybernaya provides valuable opportunities for networking, skill enhancement, and exposure to industry insights. It's a chance to connect with professionals and fellow students.

  • Who are the keynote speakers or industry experts?

    Our lineup includes esteemed keynote speakers and industry experts with diverse backgrounds. Check the schedule for detailed information on each speaker.

  • Is there a code of conduct for participants?

    Yes, we expect all participants to adhere to a code of conduct promoting respectful and inclusive behavior. Review the code of conduct on our website for detailed guidelines.

  • How can my college/organisation become a partner for Cybernaya?

    If your college/organisation is interested in partnering with Cybernaya, please reach out to us through the contact section on our website. We would love to explore collaboration possibilities.

  • How can I stay updated on Cybernaya 2023 announcements?

    Stay connected by following our official social media channels and regularly checking our website for updates and announcements.

  • Is there a dress code for the event?

    While there is no strict dress code, we recommend wearing formal attire. If there are specific dress requirements for certain events, it will be mentioned in the event details.

  • What happens if I face technical issues during the event?

    We have a dedicated technical support team to assist you. Contact the support team through the virtual platform or visit the help desk during in-person events for immediate assistance.

  • Will there be opportunities to interact with industry professionals?

    Yes, Cybernaya provides various opportunities to interact with industry professionals, including networking sessions, panel discussions, and Q&A sessions.
