Prof Vikas Pareek
Head of Department, Department of CS & IT
Mahatma Gandhi Central University, Motihari, Bihar

We are honored to welcome Dr. Vikas Pareek as the distinguished guest speaker at Cyberanya, our esteemed technical festival. With a rich academic background and over 17 years of teaching experience, Dr. Pareek stands as the Head of the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology at Mahatma Gandhi Central University in Motihari, Bihar. Holding a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Banasthali Vidyapeeth, he brings a wealth of knowledge and expertise to our event.

Dr. Pareek's illustrious career is marked by his profound insights into key areas such as Cyber Security, Mobile Computing, Computers and Society, and Theoretical Computer Science. As an accomplished scholar, his contributions to these fields are significant, making him a respected figure in the academic community.

At Cyberanya, we anticipate a captivating and enlightening session as Dr. Vikas Pareek shares his in-depth knowledge and experiences, inspiring attendees to delve into the dynamic world of computer science. His presence adds immense value to our technical festival, promising an engaging discourse on the latest advancements and challenges in the realm of technology. We look forward to the privilege of learning from Dr. Pareek's expertise and benefiting from his valuable perspectives at Cyberanya.
