Painting Tomorrow's Tech

INTRODUCTION
  • A poster representation competition which main objective is to convey complex concepts visually.
  • The main focus is on showcasing creativity and design skills.
  • Participants present ideas through visually compelling posters, expressing technical concepts in an artistic manner.
  • It enhances communication skills and the ability to convey ideas through design.
  • This intersection of technology and artistic expression promots fusion of technology, creativity, and effective communication.

JUDGING CRITERIA
  • Innovation: Originality and uniqueness of the idea.
  • Feasibility: The practicality and viability of implementing the proposed solution.
  • Practicality: Evaluation based on how the solution is practically possible on ground zero.
  • Presentation Skills: Clarity, engagement, effectiveness, and the social relevance of the presentation.
  • Social Value: Direct benefits to our society & environment.
RECOGNISATIONS & AWARDS
  • Winner: [Team with highest points] : Winner Trophy, Gold Medal, Certificate, LoR & Perks
  • Runner-Up: [Team with 2nd highest points] : Trophy, Silver Medal, Certificate, LoR & Perks
  • Most Innovative Solution: [Team with most innovative solution] : Bronze Medal, Certificate, LoR & Perks
  • Participants: Certificate
TERMS & CONDITIONS
  • Participants must strictly adhere to the specified time limits for presentations to ensure a smooth flow of the event.
  • Judges will assign scores based on the predetermined criteria. Each criterion carries a specific weightage, contributing to the overall score.
  • The decision of the judging panel is considered final. Scores and feedback may be provided to participants after the event.
  • Participants retain intellectual property rights to their ideas.
  • Participants grant permission for the use of their ideas for promotional purposes related to the event.
